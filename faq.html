FAQ's

You've got questions, and we've got the answers. Find answers to most commonly asked questions below. Still have questions? Reach out to us anytime we're here to help. Would you like any additional FAQ's added?

What is CourseMate?

CourseMate is a student-driven platform designed to help college students make informed decisions about their classes. Think of it as a mix between RateMyProfessor and LinkedIn where you can read course reviews, get insider information on professors, check textbook prices, and connect with other students!

Is CourseMate free to use?

Absolutely! CourseMate is 100% free because we believe every student should have access to helpful information without paying extra.

Who can use CourseMate?

Any college student! Whether you're a freshman planning your first semester or a senior looking to share your wisdom, CourseMate is for you.

Can I review a professor seperately from a course?

Yes! Each course page includes professor details, and you can leave feedback specifically about their teaching style, grading, and overall experience.

Are the reviews anonymous?

Yes! While you'll need an account to post, your reviews will only display your first name and last initial keeping things honest but respectful.

Does CourseMate help with textbook prices?

Yes! Each course page includes estimated textbook costs and links to affordable options so you can plan ahead and save money.
