ABOUT US!

Welcome to CourseMate, the ultimate student-powered platform built by college students, for college students!

It all started with four students who were tired of the guessing game when it came to choosing classes. We've all been there registering for a course, only to realize the professor's teaching style isn't the right fit or discovering (a little too late) that the course materials cost as much as a month's rent! That's when we thought, why isn't there a better way to prepare for classes before we even step foot in them?

So, we built CourseMate a place where students can connect, share real experiences, and get the inside scoop on classes before enrolling. Whether it'sf inding out how intense the coursework is, getting honest reviews on professors, or discovering where to snag the best deals on textbooks and materials, we wanted to create a space where students could help each other make smarter academic choices and save money along the way.

Because let's face it, college is experience. Between tuition, books, room and board, and that daily coffee habit keeping us all sane, every dollar counts. CourseMate is here to help you cut unnecessary costs, avoid surprises, and step into every semester with confidence.

So join us, start exploring, and let's build a smarter, more connected college experience together!
